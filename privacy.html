Section 1 — Categories of Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you provide directly during registration, use of the Service, inquiries, and similar processes. The categories of information collected are:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Allergen and dietary restriction profile (configured by the user)

We do not collect sensitive information. However, the allergen and dietary restriction information that you input directly may constitute health-related information; such information is used solely for the purpose of providing the Service and is not sold to any third party.

Payment Information

When you use paid services, we may collect information necessary for payment processing. Payment information is processed by external payment processors (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Stripe, and similar services); we do not store payment information directly.

Social Login Information

When you log in using a social media account, we may receive limited profile information from that platform, such as your name, email address, and profile picture.

Information Collected Automatically During App Use

The following information may be collected automatically while you use the App:

  • Location information: We may access your device's location information to provide location-based services. You may change permissions in your device settings.
  • Camera access: Camera access permission is required to provide barcode scanning and OCR functionality.
  • Device information: Technical information such as device ID, model, manufacturer, OS version, IP address, and browser type may be collected.
  • Logs and usage data: Date and time of access, features used, search terms, and error reports may be collected.
  • Push notifications: We may request push-notification permission to deliver account- or service-related notifications. You may decline in your device settings.

All personal information you provide must be accurate and complete; please notify us if anything changes.

The personal information we collect is processed for the following purposes:
